Research Correspondence Policy

Data to Impact welcomes research-related correspondence, including discussion of working papers, methodological questions, data sources, and potential collaboration.

To ensure meaningful engagement, please include:

  • A clear subject line indicating the topic of inquiry
  • Relevant context or references
  • Your institutional or professional affiliation (if applicable)

Messages that are promotional, vague, or unrelated to research may not receive a response.

Please include context in your message. Unclear emails receive delayed replies.
