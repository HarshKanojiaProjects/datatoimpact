Research

This section hosts longer-form research notes and working papers developed as part of the Data to Impact initiative.

The focus is on applied financial analysis, sustainability metrics, and data-driven evaluation of impact, with an emphasis on clear assumptions, transparent methods, and reproducible reasoning.

Current focus areas

  • Financial statement analysis and capital structure
  • ESG frameworks and sustainability metrics
  • Impact measurement and reporting
  • Data interpretation for policy and business decisions

Research outputs will be added here as they are developed. Entries may include exploratory analyses, structured notes, and draft working papers.
