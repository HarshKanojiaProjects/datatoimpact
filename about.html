About

Data to Impact is a personal research initiative focused on understanding how data, finance, and sustainability intersect in real-world decision-making.

The work here explores financial analysis, ESG metrics, and impact measurement with an emphasis on clarity, rigor, and practical relevance rather than commentary or advocacy.

This site serves as a public record of research notes, analytical frameworks, and working papers developed through independent study and applied analysis.

The intent is to document learning, test ideas, and build structured insight over time.
